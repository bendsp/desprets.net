[Garden](https://garden.desprets.net/) is a hexagonal tile puzzle game optimized for mobile use and simplicity.

I got addicted to the "Sigmar's Garden" minigame in [Opus Magnum](https://store.steampowered.com/app/558990/Opus_Magnum/),
so I decided to make a web game based on it.

The goal was to keep the rules readable, the interface minimal, and the board comfortable
to play on a phone without losing the puzzle's bite.
