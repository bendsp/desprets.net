[imagn.xyz](https://imagn.xyz/) is an AI-powered image editor and generator I built around the idea
that these tools should feel more like products and less like demos.

The app combines generation and editing in a single flow, with a stack built on
Next.js, PostgreSQL, Drizzle, Clerk, and Stripe.

It is still evolving, but the direction is clear: more capable models, a better
editing loop, and a cleaner overall experience.
