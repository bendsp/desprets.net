[Fundamental](https://www.fundamentalwallet.com/) began as a student startup I founded with five classmates in 2024
through Epitech's EIP program.

The premise was simple: crypto products had power, but most of them still felt
 too complex and too fragile for normal people to trust every day.

We wanted to build a wallet that felt obvious to use without giving up on the
core ideas of decentralization and self-custody.
