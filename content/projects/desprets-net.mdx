[desprets.net](https://desprets.net/) has been through several iterations,
including a super over-the-top terminal with interactive text backgrounds,
mainly as an excuse to use [Vercel's Pixel Geist](https://vercel.com/font/).

The current one is the simplest so far. I wanted to remove all distractions and
create something that loads as fast as possible and can be parsed by humans and
agents with minimal effort.
