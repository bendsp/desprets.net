[Bedrock](https://bedrock.desprets.net/) is a Markdown editor I started to explore a writing environment that
feels simpler, faster, and more focused than most note-taking tools.

The project sits close to the kind of software I enjoy most: text-first
interfaces, careful interaction design, and tools that stay out of the way.

I built it with React, Tailwind, TypeScript, and Electron, with a lot of
attention on editing flow and the overall feel of the app.
